# Asani QBO Automation Tool  
## Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 1st December, 2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Asani QBO Automation Tool** (“the Tool”) collects, processes, and stores data.  
The Tool is used **exclusively for internal operations** at **Asani.io**.

---

## 1. Scope of this Policy
This policy applies only to the internal Asani QBO Automation Tool and covers:
- Financial data processed through QuickBooks Online (QBO)
- Customer and transactional information retrieved or created by the Tool
- API interactions and logs necessary for internal operations

This Tool is **not available to external users**.

---

## 2. Data Processed by the Tool
The Tool may access or process the following QBO data:
- Customer details  
- Invoices, payments, sales receipts  
- Journal entries and general ledger data  
- Metadata such as IDs, timestamps, and sync logs  

The Tool does **not** collect personal data beyond what is required for normal QBO accounting operations.

---

## 3. How the Data Is Used
Data is used **solely for**:
- Automating financial workflows  
- Synchronizing accounting data between Asani.io systems and QuickBooks Online  
- Improving internal reporting and accounting accuracy  

Data is **not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes**.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security
- Access tokens and credentials are stored securely in encrypted environments.  
- Only authorized Asani.io staff may access or operate the Tool.  
- Communication with QuickBooks Online occurs over secure OAuth 2.0 and HTTPS connections.  
- Internal logs may store minimal identifiers (such as QBO IDs) for audit and debugging.

---

## 5. Data Sharing
Asani.io does **not** share Tool-processed data with third parties except:
- Cloud hosting providers (under standard confidentiality agreements)
- Legal authorities if required by law  

No data is shared for marketing, analytics, or external services.

---

## 6. Data Retention
Data is retained according to:
- Asani.io’s internal accounting policies  
- Applicable legal or regulatory requirements  

System logs may be retained for operational troubleshooting.

---

## 7. User Rights
Since the Tool is for **internal use only**, rights apply to internal staff, including:
- Right to request correction of inaccurate data entered by the Tool  
- Right to report concerns regarding data security or privacy practices  

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy
Asani.io may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Changes will be published on the hosting repository or internal communication channels.

---
